## Critical path & parallelism

- **Long pole:** RT-0 ŌåÆ RT-1 ŌåÆ RT-4 ŌåÆ RT-6 ŌåÆ RT-7 ŌåÆ RT-11 (the in-program video
  crosspoint, end to end). RT-6 carries the two heaviest verdict-mandated fixes
  (O(1) `rebind_cell` + scale-at-composite).
- **Audio lane (parallel after RT-5):** RT-8 ŌåÆ RT-9 ŌåÆ (feeds RT-11).
- **Subtitle lane (parallel after RT-5):** RT-10 ŌåÆ (feeds RT-11).
- **Output lane (gated on ADR-0030):** RT-12 ŌåÆ RT-13 ŌåÆ {RT-14, RT-15}. Until
  ADR-0030 lands, RT-12 ships the output id + `EncodedPacket` convergence + sink-
  mover against the single `"main"` program; RT-13/14/15 follow once `ProgramSet`
  exists.
- **Inventory/API lane (parallel early):** RT-2 ŌåÆ RT-3 (can land before the engine
  re-points; pure read surface, inv #10).
- **Convergence:** RT-11 (commands + #11 classifier) joins the three in-program
  lanes; RT-16 (salvo) joins RT-11 + RT-13; RT-17 (UI) is last.

## Hard acceptance gates (from adversarial review ŌĆö non-negotiable)

1. **No O(1) claim without `rebind_cell`** ŌĆö RT-6 must remove `solve_layout()` from
   the pure source re-point path; a held-out test caps per-tick hook work under a
   salvo storm.
2. **No video crosspoint without scale-at-composite** ŌĆö RT-6 must render a
   cross-geometry swap correctly (the as-built path smears).
3. **No take without WARM-ON-ARM** ŌĆö RT-7 gates every take on prime; the take frame
   is never a slate.
4. **No lip-sync claim without tick-index `bus.tick()`** ŌĆö RT-8's held-out test
   asserts zero audio drift under `DropOnOverload` + a breakaway take.
5. **No per-tick-scalar cross-fade** ŌĆö RT-9 must be a per-sample intra-block
   envelope.
6. **No encode-once claim without an encode-count test** ŌĆö RT-12 spies
   `encode_frame` call-count across a switch.
7. **No SCTE passthrough without `pts_adjustment` shift** ŌĆö RT-14 shifts by the same
   GP-6 offset or the cue misfires.
8. **#11 classifier inspects the destination's pinned params** ŌĆö RT-11 must report
   the discrete-track-layout breakaway as Class-2, not plain Class-1.
